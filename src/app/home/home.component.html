About Us

IIT Bombay has been ranked 1st in India and 49th globally in Engineering and Technology with an overall score of 79.9 out of 100 in the Quacquarelli Symonds(QS) World University Rankings by Subject for 2021. The Institute has been ranked in 4 out of 5 broad subject areas, which includes Engineering & Technology,Natural Sciences, Social Sciences and Management, and Arts and Humanities.

Education

The programmes and courses offered at the institute have the flexibility to evolve and change in response to new requirements.

Research and Development

IIT Bombay has made concerted efforts to align its R&D focus with the national goal of achieving technological self-reliance.
